Design Process

When it comes to solving problems, I like to follow a 5-step process:

  • Empathize - with the user

    • Understanding the users' needs, behaviors and usage patterns by diving deep into their minds.

  • Define - the problem

    • Gathering insights and analyzing the users' pain points to understand the problem space and formulate the final problem statement.

  • Ideate - on potential solutions

    • Brainstorming different possible ways in which the problem can be solved within existing constraints.

  • Create - viable designs

    • Concretizing ideas into solutions and defining user flows by wireframing and protototyping designs.

  • Test - for refinement

    • Testing the designs with users for identifying problem areas and iterating to further refine and improve.
