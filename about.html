I'm Ambuj (pronounced um-booj), a graduate student at UT Austin where I'm currently learning about the multiple facets of User Experience(UX) Design and Product Design. As an emerging designer in the field, I'm learning how to strike a balance between understanding user needs and aligning them effectively with the business goals of an organization while constantly trying to pick up new skills and hone my existing skills.

Though I have always had a love for all things design, the gradual shift from computer science to UX allowed me to understand how design functions at deeper levels and empowers people by solving real-world problems.

When not at school, I spend my time attending design meetups, exploring a myriad of food flavors in and around Austin and photographing sublime moments that are too good to be left uncaptured.
