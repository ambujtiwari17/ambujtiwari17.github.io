I'm Ambuj (pronounced um-buj), I'm a recent graduate of the MSIS program at the School of Information, UT Austin under the User Experience(UX) Design track. As a designer, I'm trying to learn how to strike a balance between understanding user needs and align them effectively with the business goals of an organization while constantly trying to pick up new skills and hone my existing skills.

Though I have always had a love for all things design, the gradual shift from computer science to UX allowed me to understand how design functions at deeper levels and empowers people by solving real-world problems.

When not at school, I spend my time attending design meetups, exploring a myriad of food flavors in and around Austin and photographing sublime moments that are too good to be left uncaptured.

If you're interested in getting me on board for your project, please feel free to shoot me an e-mail or message me on LinkedIn.
